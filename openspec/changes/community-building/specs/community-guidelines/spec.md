## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Community Standards
The project SHALL establish and enforce community standards for participation.

#### Scenario: Code of conduct
- **WHEN** community members interact
- **THEN** Code of Conduct SHALL define acceptable behavior
- **AND** violations SHALL have clear consequences
- **AND** reporting mechanism SHALL be provided

#### Scenario: Contribution process
- **WHEN** external contributors submit changes
- **THEN** contribution process SHALL be documented clearly
- **AND** review process SHALL be transparent
- **AND** feedback SHALL be constructive and timely

### Requirement: User Engagement
The project SHALL actively engage with users and gather feedback.

#### Scenario: Feedback channels
- **WHEN** users have questions or suggestions
- **THEN** GitHub Discussions or Issues SHALL be available
- **AND** response time SHALL be reasonable
- **AND** feedback SHALL inform development priorities

#### Scenario: Community growth
- **WHEN** promoting the project
- **THEN** project SHALL be shared on relevant platforms
- **AND** user testimonials SHALL be collected
- **AND** community metrics SHALL be tracked (stars, contributors, etc.)
